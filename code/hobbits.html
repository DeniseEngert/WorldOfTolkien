APPEARANCE
little people, about half the height of humans, even smaller than dwarfs
have no beards
inclined to be fat in the stomach
dress in bright colours, green and yellow
wear no shoes, because their feed grew leathery soles and thick, warm, brown hair
hair on their heads is also brown, but curly
long clever fingers
SKILLS
hear well
move very silently
know little to no magic
NATURE
good natured faces
laugh deep, fruity laughs, especially after dinner (which they have twice a day, when they can get it)
SOURCE: Der Hobbit (2012), Page 36
