Appearances

Hobbits are small people, about half the height of humans, even smaller than dwarves (between two and four feet). They have no beards, but
thick, brown, curly hair. They are inclined to be a bit fat around the stomach.
The colours of their clothes are bright, chiefly green and yellow. They wear no shoes, though, because their feet
developed leathery soles and thick, warm, brown hair.

Skills

They hear well, and have the ability to move very silently.
Hobbits know little to no magic. They have long, clever (skillful) fingers.

Nature

Their faces are good natured and they laugh a deep, fruity laugh, especially after dinner (which they have
twice a day, when they can get it).
